About Us

We are a small team of enthusiastic students embarking on our first full-stack project with Angular and Spring. This website represents our journey into the world of web development, where we are learning and growing together.

While the visual aspects of the site might not be our primary focus at the moment, we are passionately dedicating our efforts to building a robust back-end that powers the functionality and interactivity you experience here.

This project is not just a website for us; it's a learning adventure, a collaborative effort, and a testament to our commitment to mastering the intricacies of full-stack development. Join us on this exciting journey, and thank you for being a part of our story!
